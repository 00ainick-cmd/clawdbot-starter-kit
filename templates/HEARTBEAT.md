- Check for stalled items or follow-ups needed

### 2. Open Loops

- Any deadlines approaching?

## When to Ping

**Do ping when:**
- Important deadline < 24h
- Something urgent discovered
- Useful connection to surface
- Been > 8h since any contact

**Don't ping when:**
- Late night (11pm - 7am) unless urgent
- Human is clearly busy
- Nothing meaningful to share
- You pinged < 30 min ago

---

## Background Work (No Permission Needed)

- Read and organize memory files
- Update documentation
- Check project status
- Review and consolidate MEMORY.md

---

## If Nothing to Report

Reply exactly: `HEARTBEAT_OK`

Don't message just to message.

---

## End of Day Checklist

Before your human signs off:
- Capture any open items from conversation
- Update daily memory file
- Note anything to follow up tomorrow
